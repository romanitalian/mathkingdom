## Who we are

**Math Kingdom** (“the app”, also referred to in materials as Королевство Математики) is an **offline-first** elementary math learning app for **iPhone and iPad**.

It helps children and adults build math skills through a simple loop: **short theory → practice → session summary**. Topics include arithmetic (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division), fractions, decimals, measurement, and related elementary content bundled with the app.

The interface and lesson content are available in **eight languages**: English, Russian, French, Italian, German, Portuguese, Chinese, and Persian. **Core learning works without an internet connection**; lessons, exercises, glossary, and progress are available from data stored on your device.

This policy describes how information is handled when you use the app.

## Information we do not collect

- We **do not** require an account or sign-in.
- We **do not** ask for your name, email address, phone number, or mailing address inside the app for learning features.
- We **do not** sell your personal information.
- The app **does not** send your study progress, answers, or settings to our servers; **we do not operate a backend** that receives your learning data for the version described here.
- The **current** version includes **no in-app purchases or subscriptions** and **no third-party advertising SDKs**.

If this changes in a future version, we will update this policy and the **App Privacy** details on the App Store listing.

## Information stored on your device

To work offline and remember your place, the app stores data **locally on your device** using Apple’s app sandbox (for example **UserDefaults** and bundled JSON content), including:

- **App settings** — interface language, sound and haptic preferences, questions per session, difficulty preset, optional per-question time limit, onboarding completion, and color theme
- **Learning progress** — completed math sessions, per-topic accuracy, mastery badges, daily streak information, and which topic you last practiced
- **Session summaries (persisted)** — per-session totals, first-try correctness counts, duration, and accuracy used for stats and mastery
- **Session activity (in memory)** — during a practice session, individual exercise prompts and your answers are held on-device to show the session summary and optional mistake review; they are **not** saved to persistent storage after the session ends
- **Scheduling hints** — such as a one-time “first session” topic after onboarding or diagnostic setup

This data stays on your device under your Apple ID’s app storage. **We cannot access it remotely.** Deleting the app typically removes this local data (unless you restore from an encrypted device backup that still contains it).

## Device features the app uses

- **Sound (optional)** — short feedback sounds during practice when sound is enabled in Settings; audio files are bundled with the app, not streamed from our servers.
- **Haptics (optional)** — light vibration feedback on supported devices when enabled in Settings; handled on-device by iOS.
- **No** access to the camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, or precise location for the version described here.
